About Antigravity

Antigravity is a fascinating concept that explores the possibility of counteracting or nullifying the force of gravity. While still theoretical, it has captivated the imagination of scientists and science fiction enthusiasts alike.

Scientific Theories

Various theories have been proposed to understand and achieve antigravity. These include general relativity and quantum field theory.

Technological Concepts

Technologies such as electromagnetic levitation offer practical ways to reduce the effects of gravity, although true antigravity remains elusive.
